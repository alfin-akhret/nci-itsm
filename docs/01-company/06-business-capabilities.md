## Purpose

This document defines the business capabilities of PT Khatulistiwa Manufacturing Indonesia (KMI). Business capabilities are grouped by Business Unit to simplify ownership, service mapping, and Configuration Management. Each capability will later be mapped to one or more Business Services.

---

# Manufacturing

**Executive Sponsor:** COO

| Business Capability    | Description                                                                |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Production Planning    | Plans production schedules based on demand forecasts and inventory levels. |
| Production Execution   | Executes manufacturing operations on the production floor.                 |
| Quality Management     | Ensures products meet quality standards and regulatory requirements.       |
| Production Maintenance | Maintains manufacturing equipment and minimizes production downtime.       |

## Notes

Each Business Capability defined in this document will be mapped to one or more Business Services in the next phase of the project. Business Capabilities represent **what the business is able to do**, while Business Services describe **how those capabilities are delivered**.

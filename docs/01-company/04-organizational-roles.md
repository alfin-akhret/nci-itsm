## Purpose

This document defines the key organizational roles involved in delivering, operating, and governing IT services at PT Khatulistiwa Manufacturing Indonesia (PT KMI). These roles provide the foundation for ownership, accountability, and responsibility across Business Services, Technical Services, Configuration Items (CIs), and ITSM processes.

## Executive Roles

| Role                            | Primary Responsibility                                                 |
| ------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Chief Executive Officer (CEO)   | Provides overall business direction and executive sponsorship.         |
| Chief Information Officer (CIO) | Owns the IT strategy, digital transformation, and IT service delivery. |

## Business Roles

| Role                   | Primary Responsibility                                                     |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Business Owner         | Owns a business capability and defines business objectives and priorities. |
| Business Process Owner | Owns and continuously improves a business process.                         |
| Service Consumer       | Uses one or more business services to perform business activities.         |

## IT Service Management Roles

| Role                  | Primary Responsibility                                                                      |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Service Owner         | Accountable for the lifecycle, quality, and performance of a business or technical service. |
| Configuration Manager | Governs the CMDB, CI lifecycle, naming standards, and relationship accuracy.                |
| Change Manager        | Governs the Change Enablement process and coordinates change approvals.                     |
| Incident Manager      | Coordinates the restoration of normal service during major incidents.                       |
| Problem Manager       | Identifies root causes and drives permanent resolution of recurring incidents.              |
| IT Asset Manager      | Manages the lifecycle of IT assets from acquisition to disposal.                            |
| Service Desk Manager  | Oversees the Service Desk function and end-user support operations.                         |

## Technical Roles

| Role                         | Primary Responsibility                                             |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Application Owner            | Owns the lifecycle, roadmap, and support of an application.        |
| Infrastructure Owner         | Owns compute, storage, virtualization, and cloud platforms.        |
| Network Owner                | Owns enterprise network infrastructure and connectivity services.  |
| Database Administrator (DBA) | Manages database platforms, availability, performance, and backup. |
| Information Security Officer | Defines and enforces information security policies and controls.   |

## Notes

These organizational roles will be referenced throughout the repository when defining Business Services, Technical Services, Configuration Items (CIs), ownership, support groups, approvals, and governance.

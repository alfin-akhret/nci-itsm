## Vision
To become Indonesia’s most trusted manufacturing and distribution company by delivering high-quality products through operational excellence, digital innovation, and sustainable business practices.

## Mission
1. Deliver high-quality products consistently to customers across Indonesia.
2. Build an efficient and resilient supply chain from procurement to customer delivery.
3. Accelerate digital transformation through modern technologies, automation, and data-driven decision making.
4. Develop competent people and foster a culture of continuous improvement.
5. Maintain strong governance, compliance, and information security across all business operations.

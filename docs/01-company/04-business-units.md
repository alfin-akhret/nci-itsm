## Purpose

This document defines the major business units within PT Khatulistiwa Manufacturing Indonesia (PT KMI). Each business unit represents an organizational function responsible for delivering business capabilities and will serve as the primary owner of one or more Business Services.

| Business Unit          | Executive Sponsor | Description                                                              |
| ---------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Manufacturing          | COO               | Produces finished goods and manages factory operations.                  |
| Supply Chain           | COO               | Plans and coordinates the movement of materials across the supply chain. |
| Warehouse Operations   | COO               | Manages inventory, warehousing, and stock movements.                     |
| Procurement            | COO               | Sources raw materials and manages supplier relationships.                |
| Logistics & Fleet      | COO               | Delivers products to customers nationwide.                               |
| Sales                  | CCO               | Manages customer acquisition and sales operations.                       |
| Marketing              | CCO               | Develops marketing campaigns and product branding.                       |
| Customer Experience    | CCO               | Manages customer support and digital customer services.                  |
| Finance                | CFO               | Oversees financial planning, accounting, and reporting.                  |
| Human Resources        | CHRO              | Manages workforce planning, payroll, and employee development.           |
| Legal & Compliance     | CLCO              | Ensures regulatory compliance and legal governance.                      |
| Information Technology | CIO               | Delivers and supports enterprise technology services.                    |

## Notes

The Business Units defined in this document will be used as the primary business owners for Business Capabilities and Business Services documented in subsequent phases of the project.
